Who we are

EASY TRADERS is a general supplier and 1st class contractor located at House # 679 (2nd floor), West Kazipara, Mirpur-2, Mirpur, Dhaka-1216. Formed in 2004 by a group of professionals with extensive experience in the service sector, the organization has been enlisted with various national and international organizations. EASY TRADERS is a trusted service provider in procurement supply chains for both national and international entities.

EASY TRADERS mission

EASY TRADERS is committed to meeting customer expectations by leveraging its extensive expertise and providing consistent, high-quality services. Since 2004, the company has strived to specialize in serving diverse products and services, maintaining integrity and excellence. Its dedication to delivering significant volumes of products at competitive prices has influenced valued clients and markets, ensuring its position as a reliable team partner for leading procurement agencies.
